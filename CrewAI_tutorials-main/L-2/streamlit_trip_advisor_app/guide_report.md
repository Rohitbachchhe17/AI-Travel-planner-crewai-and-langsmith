# Itinerary for Rome (May 2026)

## Overview
Welcome to Rome! May is a fantastic month to explore the Eternal City, with pleasant weather and a variety of events and attractions. This itinerary is tailored for those interested in sightseeing and good food.

## Accommodation
- **Types of Accommodation**: 
  - Mid-range hotels (Average €150 per night)
  - Hostels
  - Airbnb apartments
- **Recommendations**: Book early to stay in the historic center.

## Day 1: Arrival & Initial Exploration
- **Morning**: Arrive at Fiumicino Airport. Take a shuttle or taxi to your hotel.
- **Afternoon**: Explore **Piazza Navona** and enjoy a gelato from **Gelateria del Teatro**.
- **Evening**: Dinner at **Trattoria Da Enzo al 29** for authentic Roman dishes.

## Day 2: Ancient Rome
- **Morning**: Visit the **Colosseum** and **Roman Forum**. The entrance fee is approximately €25.
- **Lunch**: Enjoy **Roman-style pizza** at **Pizzeria Ai Marmi**.
- **Afternoon**: Tour the **Palatine Hill**.
- **Evening**: Dinner at **Osteria Bonelli** for pasta.  

## Day 3: Vatican City
- **Morning**: Explore **St. Peter's Basilica**. Free entry, but climb the dome for stunning views (€10).
- **Lunch**: Taste local favorites at **Pizzarium** (famous for pizza al taglio).
- **Afternoon**: Tour the **Vatican Museums** (€17).
- **Evening**: Enjoy a traditional meal at **La Città dell'Altro Mondo**. 

## Day 4: Gardens & Culture
- **Morning**: Stroll through the **Borghese Gardens** (free entry).
- **Lunch**: Eat at **Café Giolitti**, famous for its desserts.
- **Afternoon**: Visit **Galleria Borghese** (reservation required, €10).
- **Evening**: Try **Ristorante Aroma**, with views of the Colosseum.

## Day 5: Festivals and Events
- **May 1st**: Participate in the **Primo Maggio concert** at Piazza San Giovanni.
- **Lunch**: Local street food at **Campo de' Fiori Market**.
- **Afternoon**: Explore various free events at the **Notte dei Musei** on May 23rd.
- **Evening**: Dinner at **Hostaria I Ponzio** for seafood specialties.

## Day 6: Shopping and Leisure
- **Morning**: Visit the **Spanish Steps** and shop at nearby boutiques.
- **Lunch**: Enjoy a light meal at **Caffè Propaganda**.
- **Afternoon**: Explore **Via del Corso** for Italian fashion.
- **Evening**: Feast on a magnificent dinner at **Ristorante Metamorfosi**.

## Events in May
- **Festa della Repubblica (June 2)**: Preparations start in late May.
- **Taste of Roma (Mid-May)**: An annual gourmet food festival.
- **Italian Open (May 6 - May 15)**: Major tennis event with various matches.

## Transportation
- **Getting Around**: Use buses or metro; tickets cost €1.50.
- **Airport Transfer**: Options include shuttles, buses, or taxi services.

## Weather
- **Average Temperatures**: 15°C to 25°C, ideal for outdoor activities.

## Budget Estimate
- **Per Day**: €30-50 for food, €15-25 for attractions, plus transportation.

## Visa Requirements
- **Visa**: Indians need Schengen visa and ETIAS authorization starting in 2026.

Enjoy your stay in Rome, relishing the rich history, delightful cuisine, and vibrant culture! Safe travels!
